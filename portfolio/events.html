Contender Approaching

Tournament | Sole Organizer, Streamer, & Graphic Designer

Contender Approaching was the first Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Weekly hosted by Contender Esports. I designed the graphics, logo (incorporating their brand), and schedule. I have personally been the on-site TO every Monday since CA 1 (February 10th, 2020). This includes running the bracket, seeding, calling matches, and managing the stream during 40+ person events. I also help market the tournament to our local scene and keep them up to date on any changes. I created QR codes and stagelists sheets on laminated paper handouts to help attendees at the event. At the event, I use a custom game branded game mods that I've created, which can be seen below. Streamed live on TV_Seven. I also cut and upload the VODs from the tournament to Springfield Smash.

Respect the Bair

Tournament | Organizer, Streamer, Graphic Designer, Bracket Manager, Marketer

Respect the Bair (incl. charity event Respect the Bair: Extralife) is a tournament series hosted by Missouri State University Esports. Within the club, I am the Smash Bros. Game Manager. This tournament series was hosted inside of Carrington Hall at Missouri State University, and was open for anyone to attend. Kyrie and I ran the bracket and organized the crew battles which took place at the event. I helped with everything, including graphics, stream, and more. Also uses a branded modpack.
