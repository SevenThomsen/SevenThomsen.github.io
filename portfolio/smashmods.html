Ozark TE

Smash Bros. Brawl

Ozark TE is a custom build of Legacy TE for Project M 3.6. This build is made with competitive play in mind but it has some quirks the local scene has agreed to or suggested. Below, you will find a list of features inside of this build.

Ozark TE has over 150 custom songs added. All songs are fitting for each stages themes, and don't take you out of the gameplay. They have all been chosen to hone you in, be it hype or focus. Below, you can find a list of the songs, along with their stage and file name in case you want to edit them. For a full song list, expand the button below.

I'm proud to say that I've worked for hours on remastering many textures in the game for higher resolution emulation. Every competitive map is revamped with high resolution textures to complement​ the gameplay. Some fighters have also been included, as well as all CSP icons, Stage Icons, and any other menu textures. These textures are in DDS texture format. Some may be made by other authors, but will not be showcased as mine.
