Scravey's Medieval Kingdoms

MC 1.7.10| 2.1.2

Scravey's Medieval Kingdoms is a modpack focused on a more surreal era. Technology mods are impressive, but the core root of Minecraft's doesn't align with these mod's goals. This modpack is all about expanding on player made factions. Magic and Medieval mods are all you will find in this pack. This modpack also focuses on a hardcore playthrough. The difficulty is increased very much, because I feel that mods can sometimes make the game only about crafting and take away the challange.
